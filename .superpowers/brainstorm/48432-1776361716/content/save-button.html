Interaction summary

  • Posts: Save button in .post-detail-actions next to the like button. Always visible.
  • Comments: Save button in .comment-header, appears on hover. Small and compact to fit the tighter space.
  • Saved state: Filled bookmark icon + blue color. On posts it says "Saved", on comments it's always visible as a quiet indicator.
  • Unsave: Click again to remove. Same toggle pattern as the like button.
